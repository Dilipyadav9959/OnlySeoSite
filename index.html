FAQ

Questions?
We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long before I see results?

Most clients see measurable improvements within 2-4 weeks. Technical SEO fixes (speed, schema, broken links) show impact immediately. GBP optimization typically drives more calls and directions within 7-14 days. Content and ranking improvements compound over 3-6 months.

Do you work with businesses outside Hyderabad?

Absolutely. While we're based in Hyderabad, we serve businesses across India — Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, Pune, and tier-2 cities. Our local SEO strategies are customized for each city's competition and search behavior.

What industries do you specialize in?

We work across healthcare, retail, hospitality, real estate, education, and professional services. Each industry gets a tailored approach — medical schema for clinics, product schema for e-commerce, event schema for hospitality, and so on.

Do I need to change my website platform?

No. We work with WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Squarespace, custom-built sites, and everything in between. Our technical SEO fixes are platform-agnostic. If your site is slow or poorly structured, we'll optimize it — not rebuild it.

What's included in the monthly retainer?

Depending on your tier: GBP management (posts, Q&A, reviews), 2-4 SEO articles, ongoing technical maintenance, monthly performance report, and a dedicated account manager. No hidden fees. No surprise charges.

How do you measure success?

We track what matters: organic clicks, search impressions, average position, CTR, GBP interactions (calls, directions, website visits), and ultimately — customer acquisition. No vanity metrics. Every number ties back to revenue.
